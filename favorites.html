FIFA NIGHT

The Showdown: FIFA NIGHT

Between 2015 and 2020, while living away from our families for our studies and work, our shared apartment became the heart of our "FIFA NIGHT" tradition. Though we gathered weekly throughout those years, the peak of this competitive chapter was between 2018 and 2020. Every Thursday, it served as a much-needed refreshment and party-like escape after a full week of classes and intensive study. What began as a simple ritual evolved into an intense 6-team competitive tournament, with this match capturing a final showdown for the title.
