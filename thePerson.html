The Person

Born in Chittagong, Bangladesh, Syed Irfan is a data scientist and softfware engineer driven by curiosity and innovation. With a BSc in Computer Science & Engineering from North South University and an MSc in Data Science from the University of Greenwich, London, he blends academic depth with real-world problem-solving.

His career spans software startups, mobile app development, and machine learning research, including a conference-published study. Syed’s passion lies in leveraging AI, deep learning, and large language models to address societal challenges and create lasting impact.


Career Summary

Syed combines technical expertise with a strategic mindset to deliver data-driven solutions that make a measurable impact. With experience in software engineering, machine learning, and project implementation, he is highly proficient in Python and skilled in translating complex data into actionable insights. He thrives in roles such as Data Scientist, Data Analyst, and Machine Learning Engineer, consistently aligning innovation with real-world needs.


Research Interest

Syed’s research focuses on Artificial Intelligence — particularly Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Large Language Models — with an emphasis on solving problems that matter. His work aims to create AI-powered solutions that support developing societies, improve quality of life, and push the boundaries of what data-driven innovation can achieve.
