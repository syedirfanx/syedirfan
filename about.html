About

Syed Md. Irfanul Alam

Also known as: Syed Irfan, Irfan

Syed Irfan was born in Chittagong, the port city of Bangladesh. After finishing his secondary schooling from Nasirabad Govt. High School securing GPA 5.0/5.0, he enrolled in Bangladesh Navy College, Chittagong for higher secondary school certification. In HSC examination Irfan secured GPA 4.3/5.0 in the science group under Chittagong Education Board. He then moved to Dhaka, and finished the Bachelor of Science in Computer Science & Engineering from North South University.

Currently, he is a Software Engineer at Codephilics. During his BS, he was an official member of Toastmasters International, and was certified for active charter member of the organization.


Career Interest

Skilled in Jupyter, Python, R programming, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Data Visualization & Analysis and critical & creative thinking.


Research Interest

My primary research interest is to ease the problems of the developing societies and empower people for better lifestyle. My primary field of interest is Artificial Intelligence (AI). Within AI, I am interested in Machine Learning, Deep Learning & Artificial Neural Network.
